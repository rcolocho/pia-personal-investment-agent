Process

Five steps from thesis to trade

No coding. No Bloomberg terminal. No 9:30am alarm. Just write what you believe and let PIA execute it.

01
Write your thesis
Plain English investment conviction — what you believe about the market over the next 12–24 months.
02
Set allocation

03

Why PIA

Two value propositions. One product.

PIA is valuable on two independent dimensions — either one alone would justify building it.

🧠
Superior pattern recognition
An LLM can identify correlations across geopolitical events, earnings data, macro cycles, sector rotations, and news sentiment simultaneously — at a breadth no individual analyst can match. It runs all the threads in parallel, every execution cycle.
Iran conflict → oil supply risk → energy rotation → risk-on momentum → broad market bid. A human analyst covers one thread. PIA runs all of them.
⚡
Emotion-free execution
The agent executes your thesis consistently every week — without panic-selling on a red Tuesday, FOMO-buying after a 30% run, or revenge-trading after a loss. The dollar allocation cap is the product's built-in risk circuit breaker.
The agent is not smarter than the market. It is more consistent than the investor. That consistency, compounded over time, is the value.
Risk Management

Guardrails baked into the product

Risk controls are enforced in the Worker code — not just advisory. Claude cannot bypass them.

🛡
Weekly allocation cap
$100/wk
Maximum the agent can deploy per execution cycle. The known, affordable risk limit baked into the product design itself.
📊
Position size limit
20% max
No single holding can exceed 20% of the portfolio. Hard-enforced before the order reaches Alpaca. Claude cannot override this.
🔴
Stop-loss enforcement
−15%
Any position down more than 15% is automatically liquidated at the start of the next execution cycle. Proceeds returned to cash.
🏦
Cash account only
No margin
Cash account type avoids the Pattern Day Trader rule. No leverage. No margin calls. You can only lose what you put in.
📋
Asset class restriction
US equities
NYSE and NASDAQ equities only in Phase 1. No options, no leveraged ETFs, no crypto. Enforced via Claude's system prompt.
⏸
One-tap pause
Instant
Toggle from the dashboard stops all future executions immediately. Existing positions stay open. Resume at any time.
